# Privacy Policy for GST Calculator India

**Last Updated:** December 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how **GST Calculator India** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection

**We do not directly collect, store, or share any personal information.** The App functions primarily offline to perform GST calculations.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

### Third-Party Services
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use:
*   **Device Identifiers:** Advertising ID (e.g., Android Advertising ID).
*   **Usage Data:** Interactions with advertisements, crash logs, and performance data.
*   **Location:** Approximate location (if permitted by your device settings) for ad targeting.

Link to privacy policy of third-party service providers used by the App:

Since we do not collect personal data directly, we do not use it.

*   Analyze ad performance and App usage.
